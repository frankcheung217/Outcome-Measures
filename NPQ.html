Northwick Park Neck Pain Questionnaire (NPQ)

This questionnaire is designed to measure how your neck pain affects your daily life.
Please choose the statement that best describes your current situation for each question.

Scoring: Each question is scored from 0 to 4. Maximum total score is 36 (or 32 if you do not drive).
The final result is shown as a percentage. Higher percentage = greater impact of neck pain on your life.
